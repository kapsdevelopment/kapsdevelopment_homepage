FAQ

Quick answers
Why is some information marked as AI-generated?

If temple descriptions and opening hours where impossible to find, information might have been generated using AI. If you spot errors, please report them so we can improve the dataset.

Why are temple names sometimes very long or cut off?

Temple names can be long in Thai. We optimize readability, but some truncation may occur in lists. The detail page shows the full context.

Where do images come from?

Images are sourced from public datasets and uploaded into storage for the app. Credits and licenses may appear in the app when available.

Can I request changes or add a temple?

Yes — easiest way is to take a picture with the app. That way we get the location of the temple also. But please, feel free to send details (name, location link, and what to change) through the contact form on this page as well. We can’t guarantee updates, but we collect requests for later releases.
