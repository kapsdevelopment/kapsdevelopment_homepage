Privacy Policy

How we collect and use information when you use the Sathu mobile app.

Applies to: Sathu mobile app Website: kapsdevelopment.com Last updated: 2026-01-19

This Privacy Policy describes how Kapsdevelopment (Ken Andersen), organization number 936 896 340, Norway (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects and uses information when you use the mobile application (the “App”).

Quick summary: We do not require accounts, do not use ads or analytics, and only access location temporarily to calculate distance to temples.

1. Scope of the App

The App helps users discover Buddhist temples in Thailand, provides informational and cultural content related to Thai Buddhism, and offers inspirational messages.

The App does not require user accounts in its current version.

2. Information We Collect

a) Location Information

The App may access your device’s precise location temporarily in order to sort temples based on distance.

  • Location data is not stored.
  • Location data is not shared.
  • Location data is used only momentarily for distance calculation.

b) User-Submitted Content

The App allows users to voluntarily submit:

  • Photos of temples
  • Associated location information (latitude and longitude)

This content is submitted for informational purposes only.

We do not intend to collect personal data through submitted images. Users are encouraged to submit images of temples or buildings only, and not images containing identifiable individuals or personal information.

Submitted content may be reviewed, stored, displayed, or removed at our discretion for quality, accuracy, or compliance purposes.

c) Contact Messages

The App includes a contact form that allows users to send messages to us voluntarily.

Users may choose to include an email address or other personal information in the message. This information is used solely to respond to the inquiry.

  • Contact messages are sent directly to us via email.
  • They are not stored in the App.
  • They are not stored in our databases.

d) Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information required for the App to function properly, such as app version or basic error logs. This information does not identify you personally.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version of the App, we do not:

  • Require user registration
  • Store names, email addresses, or user profiles
  • Track users across apps or websites
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies

4. Donations

The App contains:

  • An optional donation feature to support the developer
  • Links or buttons allowing users to donate directly to Thai temples via third-party payment providers

We do not process, store, or have access to payment information for donations made to temples. All such transactions are handled entirely by external payment providers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Supabase as a backend service provider. Supabase processes limited technical data on our behalf solely to provide App functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information or identifiable content through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information.

7. Data Retention

  • Temporary location data is not retained.
  • User-submitted content may be retained for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality or until it is reviewed or removed.
  • Contact messages are retained only within our email correspondence and only for as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to request access to or deletion of personal data.

You may contact us at any time using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly if new features are introduced. Updates will be made available within the App or on the App’s listing page.
