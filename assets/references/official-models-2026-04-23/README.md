## 5. Scrum Alliance note

Files:
- `scrum-alliance-framework-at-a-glance-download-error.xml`
- `scrum-alliance-framework-at-a-glance-image-error.xml`

Source page:
- https://resources.scrumalliance.org/Article/overview-scrum-framework

Official PDF link surfaced by the page:
- https://www.scrumalliance.org/ScrumRedesignDEVSite/media/ScrumAllianceMedia/Files%20and%20PDFs/VER5-scrum-framework_2020.pdf

What happened:
- The official article page is live and clearly references `The Scrum Framework At a Glance`.
- The browser-accessible PDF content is real.
- Automated direct download returned `BlobNotFound` on 2026-04-23, so no trustworthy local PDF or JPG could be saved from that link in this workflow.

Practical guidance:
- If you want to cite Scrum Alliance, cite the article page or manually save the PDF from a browser after verifying it opens correctly for you.
- Because the PDF itself carries a copyright notice and no open license was found in the surfaced materials, be conservative with reuse unless Scrum Alliance gives clearer permission for your use case.

Suggested citation:
- `Scrum Alliance. "The Scrum Framework At a Glance," Version 5.0, referenced via "An Overview of the Scrum Framework." Accessed April 23, 2026. https://resources.scrumalliance.org/Article/overview-scrum-framework`
